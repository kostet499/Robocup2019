NAOqi OS 2.7¶

Miscellaneous¶

NAOqi OS 2.7 modifications are:

  • Ntp has been removed in favor of timesyncd, time can now be updated at anytime. NAOqi software must use CLOCK_MONOTONIC when dealing with time.
  • Timeout after a call to reboot or shutdown has been reduced from 25s to 5s, NAOqi has 5s to stop all its services.
